◆ UIRPG — Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 30, 2026

Data We Collect

UIRPG uses Google Sign-In (OAuth 2.0) solely to enable cloud save sync. When you sign in, we receive your Google profile email address — this is used only as a player identifier to store and retrieve your game saves.

The game reads and writes a single file (uirpg_save.json) to your Google Drive's appDataFolder — a hidden folder that only this app can access. The file contains your in-game progress (level, inventory, equipment, etc.) and nothing outside the game.

Data We Do NOT Collect

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to our own servers. There are no analytics, no cookies, no tracking scripts, and no third-party services except Google's OAuth and Drive APIs. Your save data never touches any server we operate.

How Your Data Is Used

Your email and game save are used exclusively for the cloud sync feature. Without signing in, both remain entirely in your browser's localStorage and are never sent anywhere.

Data Retention & Deletion

You can delete your cloud save at any time by signing out of the game and manually removing the uirpg_save.json file from your Google Drive's app data folder (Drive settings → Manage apps → UIRPG → Delete hidden app data).

No data is retained by the developer — we have no database, no server, no way to access your saves.

Contact

For questions about this policy, contact the developer at the email associated with your Google account.
